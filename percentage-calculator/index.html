How to Calculate Percentages

In mathematics, a percentage is a number or ratio that represents a fraction of 100. It is heavily used in finance, science, and everyday problem-solving. The term comes from the Latin per centum, meaning "by a hundred".

The Core Formula

To find the percentage of a specific number, you can use the standard algebraic equation:

P × V1 = V2

Where P is the percentage (converted to a decimal by dividing by 100), V1 is the base value, and V2 is the final result.

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 2026

At LooLabs (loolabs.xyz), your privacy is our priority. We are committed to protecting any data you share while using our free online calculators. This Privacy Policy outlines what information we collect and how it is used.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

LooLabs is designed to be a frictionless, anonymous utility hub. Therefore:

  • We do not require user accounts or registrations.
  • We do not collect personal identification information (Names, Addresses, Phone Numbers).
  • The data you enter into our calculators (such as financial figures, health metrics, or math equations) is processed locally in your browser and is never saved, stored, or transmitted to our servers.

2. Information We Collect (Analytics & Cookies)

To improve our website and understand how visitors use our tools, we use Google Analytics. This service may collect non-personal, anonymous data such as:

  • Browser type and operating system.
  • Pages visited and the duration of your visit.
  • General geographic location (country/city level).

This data is aggregated and cannot be used to personally identify you.

3. Third-Party Advertisements

To keep LooLabs 100% free, we may display advertisements via third-party ad networks (such as Google AdSense). These third parties may use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to our website or other websites on the internet. You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google's Ads Settings.

4. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy occasionally to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. We encourage you to review this page periodically.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at loolabs2026@gmail.com.

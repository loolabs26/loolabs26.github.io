How Income Tax Works

In most modern economies, income tax is a progressive system. This means you do not pay a flat percentage on your entire income. Instead, your income is divided into "brackets," and each bracket is taxed at a progressively higher rate.

Marginal vs. Effective Tax Rate

Your Marginal Tax Rate is the highest bracket your final dollar falls into. Your Effective Tax Rate is the actual, mathematical percentage of your total income that you pay in taxes. Your effective rate is always significantly lower than your marginal rate.
